PIG DICE GAME

Directions on how the game is played:

The pig dice is a game that allows two users to compete against each other by rolling the dice


    Player 1 is the first to toss the dice.

    If the dice is 1 then the score of player 1 in that turn is 0 and it is player's two turn.

    If the dice is not a 1 then the player1 scores some points

    The player should pass the dice to player 2 for him also to score points

    The interesting thing about this is that the first to score 10 points wins.
